The U.S. Census Bureau's QuickFacts is a tool that provides statistics about the United States population, business, and geography. It's available for the nation, states, counties, cities, and towns with a population of more than 5,000.

  • Demographic data, such as age, sex, race, ethnicity, disability, and veteran status
  • Business data, such as the number of businesses, employment, and ownership
  • Geography data, such as population density and land area
  • Resilience data, such as poverty rate and median household income
  • Search for a state, county, city, or town by name or zip code
  • Select a topic or fact
  • View the data in tables, maps, charts, or dashboards
  • Print, embed on a website, or share via email or social media
